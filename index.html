A Timeline of Aaron Swartz's Life

  • 1986 - Born in Illinois, United States.
  • 1999 - He created the website, Theinfo.org.
  • 2000 - He became part of the working team that created the web feed format, RSS.
  • 2002 - He was one of the earliest architects of Creative Commons.
  • 2004 - He created a simple markup language called Markdown.He created the web application framework, web.py
  • 2005 - He started working with the co-founders of Reddit. In late 2005, he was given the title of co-founder for his contributions.

  • 2011- A grand jury indicted him on four counts, including wire fraud, computer fraud, and recklessly damaging a protected computer.
